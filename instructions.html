Instructions

Welcome to the Coup-Proofing Game! In this game, you will face various scenarios related to political stability and coup risk. Your goal is to make decisions that will keep your country stable and coup-proof.

You will be presented with different scenarios and two options to choose from. Select the option that you think will best help maintain stability and prevent a coup. Be careful, as some decisions might have unintended consequences.

Keep an eye on the progress bars for Stability and Coup Risk. Your objective is to maximize stability while minimizing coup risk.
